Singapore Healthy Cooking Competition

Rules and Regulations

  • Competators are given 20mins to shop for ingredients at NTUC next to PCK community center
  • Total price of ingredients should not be more than $30
  • Each team is to prepare
    1. One Pot of Soup
    2. Dishes
  • Total time spent for preparation and cooking should not exceed an hour
  • Judges decision is final
